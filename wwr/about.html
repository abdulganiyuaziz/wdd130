About Us

History

Founded in 1985, White Water Rafting began as a small family-run adventure company. Our mission has always been to bring thrill-seekers closer to nature while ensuring safety and unforgettable memories.

Over the years, we’ve expanded to offer tours on some of the most exciting rivers in the region. Today, we proudly guide thousands of adventurers each year through breathtaking landscapes and roaring rapids.
